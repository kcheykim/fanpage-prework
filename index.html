Bios

Celine Dion was born on March 30, 1968. She is a Canadian singer with a strong vocal. During the 1990's, Celine Dion recieved worldwide fame singing most songs in French and English. She was married to her late husband Rene Angelil who was her manager as well. They have three children together. Today, Celion Dion continues to be an active artist.
